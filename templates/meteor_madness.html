🔬 Understanding the Physics

  • Kinetic Energy: KE = ½mv² - determines impact severity
  • Crater Scaling: Based on empirical formulas from impact studies
  • Seismic Effects: Calculated using energy-magnitude relationships
  • Tsunami Generation: Depends on impact location and energy

🛡️ Deflection Methods

  • Kinetic Impactor: Spacecraft collision to change velocity
  • Gravity Tractor: Gravitational pull over time
  • Laser Ablation: Vaporizing surface material
  • Nuclear Deflection: Nuclear explosion near asteroid
